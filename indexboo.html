About Boo

Boo is a nauseatingly adorable toddler who is curious, and naive. She can speak, but has baby, gibberish vocabulary. The only actual words she says in the film are "Boo!", "Kitty!", and "Mike Wazowski!". In other ways, she is advanced for her age, such as already being potty trained.

Boo's characteristics

  1. Two year old Toddler
  2. Very attached to Sulley, calling him "Kitty"
  3. Has the cutest giggle
